Hi, I'm Leslie.

That's me on the left!

A Bay Area native now living in the beautiful city of Chicago.

I got my start in design and marketing at my family's coffee company. They were failing, and my dad decided to give my sister and I the reigns. It is there that I was able to "experiment" with design without any barriers from creating a new logo, social media presence, website, and overall branding. I can proudly say that between all of that and my sister's new leadership, we turned the company around and were even approached to open a second location at one of the hottest spots in downtown San Jose.

After graduating with a BA in Design Studies, I made my way to the midwest where I worked as an in-house graphic designer for, what was then, a small flower start-up.

In my free time, I can be found playing pinball, researching tech and digital design, enjoying a cold beer on a patio, or just sitting in with my cats watching TV.
