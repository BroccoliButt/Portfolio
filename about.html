Hi, I'm Leslie.

That's me on the left!

When I was in kindergarden, I remember a kid laughed at a drawing I had made because the dog had "four legs on one side"; my first critique. Clearly, I thought, the line marking the belly of the dog signified that two of the legs were indeed on the other side. (He must not have known about perspective).

Why do I remember this? I'm not sure. I'm not bitter about it. I learned an important lesson that day; everyone views art differently, and as an artist, it is important to observe, listen, learn, and grow.

Since then, I have continued to draw and continued to grow. Today, I am fueled by creativity and driven by my passion. I seek out knowledge to better my skills and practices so that I can call myself an artist and designer.
