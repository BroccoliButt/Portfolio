Poster Design / Screen Print

This is college project from a screen printing class where we had to make a poster voicing an issue on campus. While I love technology and the internet, many professors were making a move to using online services, and back in 2009, many of those services weren't done well. (Gosh, I hope it has gotten better since. It can't have gotten worse.)

Sometimes, print wins.
