Flowers for Dreams

Vehicle Wrap and Interior

As a Chicagoland staple in the online floral delivery service, Flowers for Dreams wanted to become omnipresent to their fans and give them a more hands-on interaction with the brand. After getting a flower pedaling ban lifted from the city, F4D set out to create Chicago's first floral shop on wheels.

While the truck is first meant to sell flowers, it needed to be versatile so that it could be used for various events and festivals, and also serve as a hub-and-spoke vehicle as they expand their service area. The truck also needed to be visually appealing to F4D's young demographic so that it could be organically shared on social media.
