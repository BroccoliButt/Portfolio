Girl + Dog

Photo Series

This is a project I did in college where we had to stage and/or manipulate photography. Our professor gave examples from photographers like Maggie Taylor and Gregory Crewdson, and while everyone chose to model their work from the latter, I was inspired by Julie Blackmon's work.

Each photo is an composite of many photos assembled in post production.

Leslie Ruckman
