Girl + Dog

Photo Series

This project practices staged photography and photo manipulations. Our professor gave examples from photographers like Maggie Taylor and Gregory Crewdson, and I took a particular liking to the works of Julie Blackmon.

Each photo is a composite of many photos assembled in post production and the walls were filled with a pattern to match that particular flavor of the month. I was lucky to be living with such an obedient dog!
