Heap & Honey

Logo / Website

Heap & Honey is a baking blog that is straying away from the sea of recipe websites that are filled with clutter. The logo and branding represents that cleanliness. The bulk of the brand is based off a golden hexagon shape used to create an ampersand. To compliment, I used two H's to create a honey wand with a hexagonal honey drop.

It was important that the recipe was easily accessable and easy to follow along. For that reason, I created a fixed button that can be clicked at any point to bring up the ingredient and recipe portion all on the same page. No scrolling necessary to look back at how much sugar is needed, and each list item can be checked off as you go.
