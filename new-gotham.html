New Gotham Coffee Community

Business Cards / Icons

New Gotham Coffee Community is a non-profit supporting the coffee professionals living in the Chicagoland area. From organizing educational events, holding monthly competitions, and being a local representative for the midwest coffee scene, NGCC lacked distributable items for networking.

I was asked to create business cards keeping their existing logo and making something playful that other coffee professionals will want to keep. I created a set of coffee icons mixed in with the four Chicago stars and flag colors to keep the attention on coffee and the local community.
